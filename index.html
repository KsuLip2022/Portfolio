About me

I was born in Russia. I graduated from university with a degree in Economics and Management. I worked in advertising, printing, and a press office. Later, I started my own ceramics production studio, where I created and released art. I could have been an actress or a TV host. I could have designed clothing collections, become a photographer, or a blogger creating digital content. I could have even become a system administrator, given my natural tendency to organize and optimize computer processes. But I chose to move to the best country, and in 2023 I enrolled at Arizona State University. In 2026, I’ll be graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Information Technology (Full Stack Development). My major allows me to combine all my creative, technical, and organizational skills. What could be better? I live in the sunny state of Florida. I have a husband, an adult son, two cats, and a dog. I also grow roses and keep a small garden among the palm trees.
